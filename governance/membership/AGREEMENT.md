### 3.2 Nature of Rights

| Characteristic | Description |
|----------------|-------------|
| **Non-Exclusive** | Rights are granted to all qualifying members |
| **Non-Transferable** | Rights may not be assigned or sublicensed |

## 4. Member Obligations

### 4.1 Dues Payment (Member Tier Only)

| Obligation | Requirement |
|------------|-------------|
| **Amount** | Per applicable tier and revenue band as set by Governing Board |
| **Invoice Date** | Upon approval (initial) or anniversary (renewal) |
| **Payment Due** | Within thirty (30) days of invoice |
| **Late Payment** | Sixty (60) day grace period; rights suspended at day 60 |
| **Non-Payment** | Automatic downgrade to Observer at day 90 |

### 4.2 Organizational Representatives

The Member shall:
- Designate a Primary Contact to receive official communications
- Designate a Secondary Contact as backup
- Designate Voting Representative(s) authorized to vote on Member's behalf
- Notify Foundation within fourteen (14) days of any representative changes
- Ensure representatives have authority to bind the organization

### 4.3 Code of Conduct

The Member agrees to:
- Comply with the [Code of Conduct](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- Ensure all representatives and employees participating in Foundation activities comply
- Cooperate with Code of Conduct investigations
- Accept enforcement decisions subject to appeal rights

### 4.4 Contact Information

The Member shall:
- Maintain accurate contact information on file
- Notify Foundation within fourteen (14) days of changes to:
  - Legal entity name or structure
  - Principal place of business
  - Contact persons
  - Billing information

### 4.5 Trademark Usage

The Member shall:
- Use Foundation trademarks only as permitted by Trademark Policy
- Not imply endorsement beyond membership status
- Cease trademark use upon membership termination
- Not register confusingly similar marks

All contributions to the Standard must be submitted with a Developer Certificate of Origin (DCO) sign-off. By signing off, contributors certify:

### 5.3 Patent Grant

Per Apache License 2.0 Section 3:
- Contributors grant a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free patent license
- For patent claims necessarily infringed by their contributions

### 5.4 No Additional IP Claims

Membership does not:
- Grant any intellectual property rights beyond Apache 2.0 license
- Create any ownership interest in the Standard
- Provide exclusive rights to any specifications
- Transfer any Foundation intellectual property

### 5.5 Member Intellectual Property

The Foundation does not claim any rights to Member's:
- Pre-existing intellectual property
- Implementations of the Standard
- Products or services using the Standard
- Trade secrets or confidential information

## 7. Term and Renewal

### 7.1 Initial Term

The initial membership term is one (1) year from the Effective Date.

### 7.2 Renewal

| Aspect | Policy |
|--------|--------|
| **Automatic Renewal** | Membership renews automatically for successive one-year terms |
| **Renewal Notice** | Invoice serves as renewal notice (sent 30 days before anniversary) |
| **Opt-Out** | Written notice required at least thirty (30) days before renewal |
| **Rate Changes** | New rates apply at renewal; notice provided with invoice |

### 7.3 Termination by Member

The Member may terminate this Agreement:
- At any time with thirty (30) days written notice
- Notice to: membership@galileoprotocol.io
- Effective at end of notice period or current term (whichever later, unless waived)

### 7.4 Termination by Foundation

The Foundation may terminate this Agreement:

**For Cause:**
- Material breach of this Agreement (with 30-day cure period if curable)
- Violation of Code of Conduct resulting in permanent ban
- Non-payment beyond grace period
- Fraudulent revenue band reporting
- Antitrust violation

**Immediate Termination (no cure period):**
- Actions threatening legal liability of Foundation
- Criminal conduct related to participation
- Severe Code of Conduct violations

### 7.5 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:
- Member rights under this Agreement cease
- Apache 2.0 specification license continues (independent of membership)
- Member must cease use of "Member" designation
- Member must cease use of Foundation trademarks
- No refund of prepaid dues (except as provided in Section 6.4)
- Confidential information obligations survive

## 8. Confidentiality

### 8.1 Foundation Confidentiality Obligations

The Foundation shall treat as confidential:
- Member contact information (not published without consent)
- Revenue band self-reporting (never disclosed)
- Voting records (individual votes not published)
- Closed session discussions

### 8.2 Member Confidentiality Obligations

The Member shall treat as confidential:
- Closed session discussions
- Draft specifications not yet published
- Security vulnerability reports during disclosure period
- Other information marked "Confidential" by the Foundation

### 8.3 Permitted Disclosures

Confidential information may be disclosed:
- With written consent of the disclosing party
- To legal counsel under confidentiality obligation
- As required by law or court order (with notice if permitted)
- In summary form that does not identify the source

### 8.4 Survival

Confidentiality obligations survive termination for three (3) years.

## 10. Antitrust Compliance

### 10.1 Acknowledgment

The Member acknowledges that participation in the Galileo Luxury Standard is subject to antitrust and competition laws. The Member has reviewed and agrees to comply with the Antitrust Compliance provisions in [CHARTER.md](../CHARTER.md) Section 9.

### 10.2 Permitted Activities

The Member may participate in:
- Development and discussion of technical specifications
- Sharing best practices for specification implementation
- Coordination on data formats and interoperability
- Establishment of conformance and certification criteria

### 10.3 Prohibited Activities

The Member shall not engage in discussions regarding:
- **Pricing:** Current, future, or historical prices; pricing strategies; price components
- **Market Allocation:** Division of customers, territories, or markets
- **Boycotts:** Agreements to refuse dealing with competitors, suppliers, or customers
- **Competitive Intelligence:** Sharing of non-public competitive information
- **Production:** Agreements on production levels or capacity

### 10.4 Compliance Procedures

The Member agrees to:
- Attend antitrust briefings as required
- Object to and leave discussions that venture into prohibited territory
- Report potential violations to the Governing Board
- Cooperate with any antitrust compliance investigation

### 10.5 Consequences

Antitrust violations may result in:
- Immediate membership termination
- Exclusion from all Foundation activities
- Referral to appropriate regulatory authorities
- No limitation on Foundation's right to seek legal remedies
